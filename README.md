## Project Overview
With the rapid advancement of technology, the healthcare sector faces major challenges in managing and preserving patient medical records. Many healthcare institutions still rely on traditional paper-based records, which leads to information loss, difficulty in access, delayed medical procedures, weak data sharing between patients and healthcare providers, and increased risk of medical errors.

MediTrack is a digital Personal health Record (PHR) system designed to allow patients to securely manage, update, and share their health information. The system helps improve healthcare service quality and supports faster and more accurate medical decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives
- Create a unified electronic database for individual patient health records.
- Enable patients to easily manage and track their medical data.
- Provide healthcare providers with fast and secure access to patient information.
- Reduce repeated medical tests and errors caused by missing information.
- Enhance data security and privacy using secure authentication mechanisms.

---

## Features
- Patient health record management (medical history, medications, test results)
- Medical document upload and storage
- Controlled data sharing with healthcare providers
- Secure access and privacy-focused design

## Results
The system successfully provides patients with the ability to register, update, and manage their personal medical records digitally. Authorized healthcare providers can securely access patient records, improving the speed and accuracy of healthcare services. MediTrack enhances data privacy, supports informed medical decisions, and demonstrates the effectiveness of digital health record systems.
